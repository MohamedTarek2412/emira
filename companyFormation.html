Free Zone Company Formation

Free Zones are like a playground for businesses—tax-free, fast setup, and tailored for specific industries. Perfect if you’re into tech, trading, or media!

Why Go Free Zone?

  • Zero Taxes: No corporate or personal taxes, plus no customs duties.
  • 100% Ownership: You’re the boss, no local sponsor required.
  • Industry Hubs: Places like Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA) or Dubai Media City are built for specific sectors.
  • Easy Setup: Pre-packaged solutions make starting up a breeze.

Mainland Company Formation

Want to dive into the UAE’s local market? A Mainland company gives you total freedom to trade anywhere, snag government contracts, and grow big!

Why Choose Mainland?

  • Unlimited Market Access: Trade directly with UAE consumers and businesses, no middleman needed.
  • Government Contracts: Bid on big-ticket government projects to boost your revenue.
  • Location Flexibility: Set up shop anywhere in the UAE and open multiple branches.
  • 100% Ownership: Full control in most sectors, no local sponsor for most activities.
  • Over 3,000 Activities: From retail to healthcare, pick what suits your business.

Mainland Licenses

Choose from three types:

  • Professional: For services like consulting or gyms, issued by the DED.
  • Commercial: For trading goods, retail, or construction.
  • Industrial: For manufacturing, with extra approvals from other authorities.

Offshore Company Formation

Looking for privacy and tax savings for international business? An Offshore company is your go-to, with no need to set foot in the UAE!

Why Go Offshore?

  • Zero Taxes: No taxes on profits, capital gains, or income—keep what you earn.
  • Privacy First: Strict confidentiality laws protect your identity and assets.
  • 100% Remote: Set up and manage your company from anywhere in the world.
  • Asset Protection: Safeguard your wealth with a secure offshore structure.
  • Key Jurisdictions: Choose from JAFZA, DIFC, or RAK ICC for cost-effective setups.
