Who We Are

Our Story

At Proemirazone, we’re a team of passionate professionals with deep expertise in Dubai’s business setup and consultancy landscape. Founded by Zakaria Eeckhout, who moved from Belgium to escape high taxes and complex regulations, Proemirazone was born from a desire to simplify the process for entrepreneurs worldwide. Our understanding of UAE’s market, regulations, and opportunities ensures you get top-tier advice and support.

Our Vision

We aim to be Dubai’s leading business setup consultancy, known for excellence, integrity, and client success. By empowering entrepreneurs to thrive in the UAE’s vibrant economy, we’re shaping a future where your business dreams become reality with ease and confidence.

Our Founder

Zakaria Eeckhout

Growing up in Belgium, Zakaria Eeckhout could’ve stayed and built a life there, but the heavy tax burden—losing nearly half his earnings—didn’t align with his vision for a thriving business. After relocating to Dubai, he navigated the complex process of decertifying his old business and setting up a new one, wishing for a service to guide him. That’s when Proemirazone was born. Zakaria’s journey fuels our mission to make business setup in Dubai seamless, efficient, and tailored for entrepreneurs like you.

“I wanted a service that could simplify this process. Now, Proemirazone is that service, helping you start strong in the UAE.”
