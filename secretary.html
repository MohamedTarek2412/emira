Visa Services

Simplify your UAE visa process with our comprehensive sponsorship solutions.

Expert Guidance

Our team provides up-to-date advice on UAE visa regulations, ensuring compliance and a smooth process.

Hassle-Free Process

We manage every step of the visa application, reducing your administrative burden.

Personalized Support

Tailored visa solutions for employees, families, or investors, designed to meet your unique needs.

Employment Visa Sponsorship

Attract top talent with our end-to-end employment visa services, including eligibility checks, document preparation, application submission, and renewals (15-20 working days).

Requirements: Passport copy (6+ months validity), recent photo, signed offer letter.

Family/Dependent Visa Sponsorship

Sponsor your spouse, children, parents, or domestic workers with our streamlined process (7-10 working days). We handle document collection, medical exams, and Emirates ID applications.

Requirements: Emirates ID, passport copies, legalized marriage/birth certificates, salary certificate.

Investor Visa Sponsorship

Secure residency through company or real estate investments (15-20 working days). We provide investment consultation and documentation support for investments starting at AED 70,000 (company) or AED 750,000 (property).

Requirements: Trade license, title deed, or bank letter for AED 2M fixed deposit.

Digital Nomad Visa

Live and work remotely in Dubai with a 1-year renewable visa (10-12 working days). Ideal for remote workers earning USD 3,500+/month, with no local sponsor required.

Requirements: Proof of employment, salary, health insurance, bank statements.

Golden Visa

Enjoy 10-year residency for investors, professionals, or property owners (10-12 working days). Benefits include self-sponsorship and family sponsorship with no frequent entry requirements.

Requirements: Proof of investment, qualifications, or academic achievements.

Freelance Visa

Operate legally as a freelancer in Dubai with a cost-effective resident visa (10-12 working days). Ideal for consultants, influencers, and self-employed professionals.

Requirements: Passport copy, photo, CV, current visa (if applicable).
